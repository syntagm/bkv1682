Reserve Selection

The Blaauwklippen Reserve Collection is the ultimate expression of selected terroir blocks. Only the finest grapes are hand selected, spontaneously fermented and matured in French oak barriques for at least 18 months, resulting in wines with good structure with firmer, finer grained tannins, ideal for cellaring purposes. The Reserve Collection is produced only when the quality of our fruit is of the highest quality, therefore availability will be vintage dependent.

Nobel Late Harvest

Our Noble Late Harvest dessert wines neatly round off any meal with their sweet, full-bodied character. This is achieved by a long and rich over-ripeness of the grapes and the resulting high must weight that gives these wines their characteristic flavour.

Vineyard Selection

Our premium wines are made from only the highest quality red grapes, matured in small oak barrels. These exclusive wines are the very best that Blaauwklippen has to offer and represent our cellar master's finest barrel selection.

Cultivar Selection

The wines in our Cultivar Selection reflect the typical characteristics of each cultivar. This is achieved as a result of the great care with which the grapes are handled from vineyard to cellar. This allows the true character and complexity of each variety to emerge in the large oak vats.
